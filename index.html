Wildland Saga

A Community-Driven Dungeons and Dragons Game

What is Wildland Saga?

A dangerous, unexplored island, avoided by all but those who would put their lives on the line for fame and riches... or those looking for adventure. Who knows what you might find here?


Wildland Saga is a community-driven game for D&D 5th edition. Set in a grim, norse-inspired setting, the players try to build a settlement in hostile lands, while roaming those same lands in search for ancient treasures.
